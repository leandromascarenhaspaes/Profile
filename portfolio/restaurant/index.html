Why our Napoletana Pizzas are so special?

The Napoletana Pizzas were listed by UNESCO as an Intangible and Cultural Heritage of Humanity and follow the strict rules of the Italian STG (Specilità Tradizionale Garantita). This means that we produce pizzas in a very special way, with a unique flavor and full of lightness.

Made only of Italian top flour 00, water, yeast, salt, time, and a lot of passion, our dough rests and ferments for at least 48 hours, that's why so much flavor and lightness. To maintain its unique characteristics, it is roasted in a wood-fired Napoletano oven that reaches 896°F (480°C), which leaves my edges full of Leopardatas (black spots on the edges).
